## Design Context

### Users
The site serves two audiences: prospective parents comparing preschools in Wrocław and families whose children already attend Wesołe Nutki. The homepage should prioritize prospective parents and convert their interest into a phone call or contact inquiry, while keeping schedules, news, documents, and other practical information easy for current families to reach.

### Brand Personality
Warm, playful, trustworthy, educational, intimate, and premium. The experience should feel welcoming to children and reassuring to parents without becoming childish, institutional, or exclusive.

### Aesthetic Direction
A bright, tactile, music-inspired preschool identity with friendly imagery and joyful details, grounded by calm typography, clear information hierarchy, and concrete trust signals. The visual character should combine family warmth, educational credibility, and the personal attention of a smaller premium preschool. The primary theme is light and approachable.

### Design Principles
1. Make calling or contacting the preschool the unmistakable primary conversion path.
2. Lead prospective parents from emotional reassurance to concrete evidence, then to a low-friction inquiry.
3. Keep high-frequency information for current families immediately findable without competing with the primary conversion.
4. Balance playful musical personality with legibility, credibility, and calm decision-making.
5. Use specific proof—people, facilities, program details, location, and real outcomes—rather than generic promotional claims.
