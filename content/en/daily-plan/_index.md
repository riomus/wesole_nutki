## **6:30 AM – 8:10 AM**

Children arrive at preschool. Free play in activity corners. Teachers' work of corrective-compensatory, stimulating, and observational nature.

## **8:10 AM – 8:20 AM**

Active games or morning exercises.

## **8:20 AM – 8:30 AM**

Preparation for breakfast, tidying activities. Hygiene procedures.

## **8:30 AM – 8:50 AM**

Breakfast.

## **8:50 AM – 9:00 AM**

Self-care activities.

## **9:00 AM – 11:30 AM**

Program activities inspired by the teacher to support children's development in linguistic, cognitive, social, artistic, physical, and health spheres. Time spent outdoors: free play, physical activities, sports, nature activities, conducting observations, educational or recreational trips and walks.

## **10:00 AM – 10:30 AM**

Free play, integration games.

## **11:30 AM – 11:40 AM**

Self-care activities, hygiene procedures.

## **11:40 AM – 12:05 PM**

Lunch (soup).

## **12:05 PM – 1:30 PM**

Toddlers: rest time on cots.

## **12:05 PM – 12:30 PM**

Older groups: relaxation activities, quiet time.

## **12:30 PM – 1:30 PM**

Additional educational activities. Free activities in the classroom or in the garden.

## **1:30 PM – 1:40 PM**

Self-care activities, hygiene procedures.

## **1:40 PM – 2:00 PM**

Lunch (main course).

## **2:00 PM – 3:30 PM**

Teachers' work of corrective-compensatory, stimulating, and observational nature. Board games with small groups of children, educational games and activities. Play in activity corners according to children's choice. Free play in the preschool garden. Additional paid activities.

## **3:30 PM – 3:40 PM**

Self-care activities, hygiene procedures.

## **3:40 PM – 4:00 PM**

Afternoon snack.

## **4:00 PM – 5:30 PM**

Storytelling or reading fairy tales. Board games, table activities with small groups of children. Free play in the classroom or in the preschool garden. Individual contacts with parents.
