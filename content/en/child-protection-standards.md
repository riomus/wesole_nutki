At our preschool, the most important thing is that every child feels safe, happy, and cared for. That's why we implement additional child protection standards that help us care for their safety and well-being. Although these standards stem from applicable legal regulations, they are above all an expression of our responsibility for the children you entrust to us.

Our preschool is a place where every child can develop in an atmosphere of trust, warmth, and respect. We ensure that our teachers are properly prepared and aware of how important it is to protect the youngest.

We invite you to familiarize yourself with the new rules and procedures we are implementing to jointly protect a safe and joyful place for all children at our preschool. We believe that together we can provide them with the best conditions for learning, playing, and growing in a friendly atmosphere.
