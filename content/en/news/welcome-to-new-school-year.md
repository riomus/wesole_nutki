We are thrilled to welcome all children and parents to the new school year 2024/2025!

## What's New This Year?

This school year we have prepared many new activities:

- **Music classes** - twice a week
- **English language learning** - through play and songs
- **Art classes** - developing creativity
- **Outdoor activities** - daily walks and garden play

## First Week Schedule

The first week will be full of adaptation and getting to know each other. We encourage parents to be patient and cooperative.

We wish everyone a successful school year!
