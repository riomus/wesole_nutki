We want to create a place that is friendly to children, parents, teachers, and all preschool staff. Our priority is to ensure that children feel safe and accepted as they develop their interests, learn through play and action, and acquire skills and knowledge according to their individual developmental path. Our goal is to raise children to live in harmony with themselves, with other people, and with the surrounding world, while preparing them to start school.

We are aware of the importance of children's contact with nature, so we take every opportunity to spend time in our garden, which not only has a functional playground but will soon also be equipped with vegetable gardens managed by individual groups.

The "Wesołe Nutki" Preschool is located in the heart of Wrocław, which enables us to cooperate with numerous cultural institutions, such as the Capitol Musical Theater, Wrocław Puppet Theater, Wrocław Opera, and Helios Cinema. We organize numerous trips and actively participate in the life of the local community.

We also encourage you to visit other sections of our website, such as STAFF or OFFER, to get to know us better.
