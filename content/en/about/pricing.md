**Payment Principles for the 2025/2026 School Year**

[**Payment Regulations**](https://wesolenutki.eu/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/REGULAMIN-odplatnosci-przedszkole-Wesole-Nutki-2025-2026-2.pdf)

The registration fee is paid once and amounts to 900 PLN.

**Monthly tuition is 900 PLN.**

The meal fee is 25 PLN/day for a standard diet or 27 PLN/day for a special diet.

Tuition includes program activities (3 times a week English language classes, 2 times a week general development gymnastics with corrective elements, 2 times a week rhythmics, once a week logorhythmics) as well as events organized at the preschool, including theater performances and concerts.

The tuition amount for the next school year is determined by the end of February.
