## About Kindergarten

Kindergarten at Wesole Nutki is an intensive year of school preparation. Our program ensures that every child starts elementary school with full readiness - both in terms of knowledge and social skills.

## Year-Long Preparation Program

### Language Arts
- **Reading** - independent reading of simple texts, comprehension
- **Writing** - correct writing of letters, syllables, and simple words
- **Speaking** - constructing statements, storytelling, discussions
- **Listening** - following instructions, understanding commands

### Mathematics
- Counting up to 100
- Addition and subtraction up to 10
- Solving word problems
- Geometric shapes and their properties
- Measuring and comparing
- Paper orientation

### World Knowledge
- Nature - plants, animals, ecosystems
- Human body - structure, health
- Technology - how things around us work
- Society - professions, institutions
- History - traditions, holidays, important events

## Assessment and Support

### School Readiness Assessment
We conduct regular assessment covering:
- Cognitive skills
- Social-emotional development
- Motor skills
- Readiness for reading and writing

### Individual Support
- Catch-up sessions for children needing support
- Enrichment program for gifted children
- Consultations with psychologist and speech therapist
- Regular parent meetings

## Additional Activities

- **Chess** - developing strategic thinking
- **Robotics** - programming and construction
- **Theater** - developing expression and confidence
- **Sports** - soccer, gymnastics, dance
- **Music** - instrument lessons, choir singing

## School Preparation

Our graduates:
- ✓ Read simple texts independently
- ✓ Write letters and numbers
- ✓ Count and perform simple operations
- ✓ Can work independently and in groups
- ✓ Know basic English
- ✓ Are emotionally ready for new challenges

## Parent Partnership

We offer:
- Monthly meetings and consultations
- Individual discussions about child's progress
- Parent workshops
- Access to online reports and assessments
- Joint events and celebrations
