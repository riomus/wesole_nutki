## About Pre-K Group

The Pre-K Group is a time of intensive preparation for school. Our program combines solid educational foundations with the joy of learning and play. Children develop skills that will allow them to confidently start elementary school.

## Educational Program

### Reading and Writing Preparation
- Syllable method - learning to combine syllables
- Recognizing and writing letters
- Graphomotor exercises
- Listening and storytelling
- Vocabulary development

### Mathematics
- Counting up to 20
- Simple operations - addition and subtraction
- Geometric shapes
- Spatial orientation
- Solving mathematical problems

### World Knowledge
- Nature and ecology
- Science experiments
- Geography and learning about cultures
- Astronomy for children
- Research projects

## Additional Activities

### Chess
Chess learning develops:
- Logical thinking
- Strategic planning
- Concentration and patience
- Ability to predict consequences

### Robotics and Programming
Introduction to the world of technology:
- Simple educational robots
- Basics of visual programming
- Problem solving
- Teamwork on projects

### English Language
Intensive course with native speaker:
- Daily language classes
- Conversations and games
- Bilingual projects
- Preparation for continuing learning at school

## School Readiness

We prepare children for school through:
- Regular progress assessment
- Individual support in areas requiring work
- Parent cooperation
- Developing independence and responsibility
- Building positive attitude toward learning
