## About Preschool Group

The Preschool Group is a time of intensive development and world discovery. Children at this age are full of energy and curiosity - we harness this by offering a rich program of activities developing all areas of child development.

## Educational Program

Our program includes:

### Cognitive Development
- Letter and number recognition
- Simple mathematical operations
- Learning about nature and surroundings
- Experiments and experiences

### Artistic Development
- Art activities with various techniques
- Music and singing
- Dance and creative movement
- Theater and drama

### Social-Emotional Development
- Group work
- Conflict resolution
- Expressing emotions
- Building friendships

## English Language

We introduce English language learning through:
- Daily songs and rhymes
- Games and language activities
- Simple conversations
- Fairy tales and stories in English

## Why Choose Us?

- Age and ability appropriate program
- Learning through play and experience
- Regular additional activities
- Individual approach to each child
- Preparation for the next stage of education
