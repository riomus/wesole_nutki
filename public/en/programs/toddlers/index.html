Toddler Group

First step into preschool adventure. Gentle introduction to group life, developing independence and basic social skills.

About Toddler Group

The Toddler Group is the perfect place for children starting their preschool adventure. We understand how important this first step is, which is why we provide a warm, safe atmosphere and individual attention to each child.

Our Approach

In the Toddler Group, we focus on:

  • Gentle adaptation - helping children adjust to the new environment at their own pace
  • Building independence - supporting children in acquiring basic self-care skills
  • Building relationships - teaching how to make first friendships and cooperate with peers
  • Sensory play - stimulating development through various sensory experiences

Why Choose Us?

Our preschool offers:

  • Qualified staff experienced in working with the youngest children
  • Small groups ensuring individual attention
  • Safe, age-appropriate teaching materials
  • Regular contact with parents and progress reports
  • Flexible hours adapted to family needs
