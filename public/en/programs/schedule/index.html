Daily Schedule

Our preschool day is carefully planned to provide children with optimal conditions for learning, play, and rest. The schedule is flexible and adapted to the needs of different age groups.

🌅 Morning

7:00 - 8:30

Arrival & Free Play

Children arrive and engage in quiet activities, free play in learning centers, individual conversations with teachers.

8:30 - 9:00

Breakfast

Healthy breakfast prepared fresh daily. Children learn table manners and self-service skills.

9:00 - 9:30

Circle Time

Morning greeting, calendar activities, discussing the day's plan, songs and movement activities.

📚 Learning Time

9:30 - 10:30

Main Educational Activity

Structured learning activities based on weekly theme: literacy, math, science, art, or social studies. Small group and individual instruction.

10:30 - 11:30

Outdoor Play & Physical Activity

Structured outdoor play, sports activities, nature exploration. In bad weather: indoor gym or movement activities.

☀️ Midday

11:30 - 12:00

Lunch

Nutritious hot lunch prepared by our kitchen. Balanced meals following children's dietary needs and preferences.

12:00 - 14:00

Rest Time

Nap time for younger children. Older children engage in quiet activities: reading, puzzles, art projects.

🌤️ Afternoon

14:00 - 14:30

Afternoon Snack

Light healthy snack: fruits, vegetables, dairy products, whole grain snacks.

14:30 - 15:30

Enrichment Activities

Specialized activities: music, dance, English, art workshops, science experiments, or special events.

15:30 - 17:00

Free Play & Departure

Free play time, learning centers, outdoor play (weather permitting). Gradual pickup by parents with opportunity for teacher communication.

🍎

Meals & Nutrition

All meals are prepared fresh daily in our kitchen using high-quality ingredients. We accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies.

🎒

What to Bring

Comfortable clothes for play, indoor shoes, change of clothes, and any comfort items for rest time (for younger children).

📱

Stay Connected

Regular updates through our parent app, daily reports, and open communication with teachers during drop-off and pick-up times.
