Autumn Family Picnic

♪●♫
Events
15 October 2024 1 minutes read

We warmly invite all families to our annual autumn picnic!

When and Where?

Date: October 20, 2024 (Sunday) Time: 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM Location: Wesole Nutki preschool garden

Event Program

  • 11:00 AM - Opening and welcome
  • 11:30 AM - Team games for children and parents
  • 12:30 PM - Refreshments (please bring one dish for the shared table)
  • 1:30 PM - Family competitions with prizes
  • 2:30 PM - Dance performance by our preschoolers

Please confirm your attendance by October 15.
