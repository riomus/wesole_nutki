Cooking Workshops for Children

5 2024 1 minutes read

We are introducing a new initiative - cooking workshops for our preschoolers!

About the Workshops

Cooking workshops are a great way to:

  • Learn healthy eating habits
  • Develop manual skills
  • Explore different flavors and textures
  • Have fun together and integrate

Schedule

Workshops will be held every two weeks, on Fridays:

  • November 8 - Healthy sandwiches
  • November 22 - Fruit salad
  • December 6 - Christmas gingerbread cookies
  • December 20 - Healthy snacks

All ingredients are provided. Please inform us of any food allergies.
