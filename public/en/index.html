FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Didn't find your answer? We'd love to talk.

Book a visit →
What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday to Friday, 6:30 AM–5:30 PM.

How much does it cost?

Tuition is 900 PLN per month. Meals are 25 PLN/day (standard) or 27 PLN/day (special diet). Tuition covers all the program activities.

What's included in the program?

Daily music and English, plus rhythmics, logorhythmics, developmental gymnastics, and concerts and shows held at the preschool.

What does settling in look like?

Small, intimate groups allow a gentle adaptation at each child's own pace. We know every child and family, and a steady daily rhythm builds a sense of safety.

How do I enroll my child?

Call or write, book a visit, and download the enrollment form. We describe the whole process step by step on the Enrollment page.
