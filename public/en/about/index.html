About Us

Get to know Wesole Nutki - a place where every child discovers joy in learning

Wesole Nutki preschool is a place where every child is special. Our team of qualified educators ensures the comprehensive development of children in a safe and friendly atmosphere.

Our Mission

We believe every child has unlimited potential. Our mission is to support children’s natural curiosity and develop their talents through creative play and modern educational methods.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced and dedicated teaching staff
  • Small groups ensuring individual attention
  • Modern educational materials
  • Safe and colorful classrooms
  • Our own playground
  • Healthy, home-cooked meals

Our Mission

What drives us every day

At Wesole Nutki, we believe every child has unlimited potential. Our mission is to support children's natural curiosity and develop their talents through creative play and modern educational methods in a warm, family atmosphere.

  • Individual approach to every child
  • Learning through music and languages
  • Safe and supportive environment
  • Experienced and passionate educators

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Safety First

We provide a secure, monitored environment where children can explore and grow with confidence.

Creative Expression

Through music, art, and play, we encourage children to express themselves freely and develop their imagination.

Holistic Development

We focus on developing the whole child - cognitive, emotional, social, and physical skills.

Strong Community

We build lasting relationships between children, families, and educators, creating a supportive network.

Respect & Kindness

We teach children to respect others, embrace diversity, and show kindness in all interactions.

Joy of Learning

We believe learning should be joyful, inspiring a lifelong love of discovery and exploration.
