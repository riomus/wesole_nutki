About Us

Discover Wesole Nutki - a preschool where every child is special

Wesole Nutki preschool is a place where every child is special. Our team of qualified educators ensures the comprehensive development of children in a safe and friendly atmosphere.

Our Mission

We believe every child has unlimited potential. Our mission is to support children’s natural curiosity and develop their talents through creative play and modern educational methods.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced and dedicated teaching staff
  • Small groups ensuring individual attention
  • Modern educational materials
  • Safe and colorful classrooms
  • Our own playground
  • Healthy, home-cooked meals

Our Mission

What makes us unique

Wesole Nutki is a place where every child discovers their potential in an atmosphere full of warmth and acceptance. Our goal is to support the comprehensive development of children through play, creativity, and an individual approach to each little one.

  • Individual approach to every child
  • Learning through play and experience
  • Safe and friendly environment
  • Supporting children's natural curiosity

Our Values

The foundations of our work with children

Safety

We provide a safe space where children can freely develop and explore.

Creativity

We inspire children to create, experiment, and express their imagination.

Development

We support the harmonious intellectual, emotional, and social development of every child.

Community

We build a friendly community of parents, teachers, and children based on mutual respect.

Respect

We teach children respect for themselves, others, and the world around them.

Joy

We create an atmosphere full of laughter, joy, and positive energy.
