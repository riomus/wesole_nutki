Offer - Wesołe Nutki

Posted on 23 2026

Our preschool cares for children aged 3-6 years (2.5-year-olds are accepted) in five groups. It operates in a detached, single-story building located in close proximity to Wrocław’s Market Square. The classrooms are equipped with furniture and toys adapted to the needs and age of the children. Pastel colors give the interiors a warm and cheerful atmosphere.

The preschool garden and the playground located in it provide joyful outdoor play. We pay special attention to ensuring that children spend as much time as possible in the garden and on walks, even in less favorable weather. The playground is equipped with two climbing sets with slides, a “monkey bars” climbing set for the oldest children, a train, a double sandbox, spring rockers, and a chalkboard for drawing.

Nutrition. Quality is our priority. Meals are prepared especially for us by the modern kitchen of Kamienica pod Aniołami (Angels’ Tenement House) at ul. Kazimierza Wielkiego 31-33, which belongs to the operating body. We use high-quality products, serve plenty of fresh vegetables and fruits, and avoid using sugar. We employ dietitian Jolanta Link, who ensures proper nutritional balance for our students and that the meals are tasty and attractive to children.

Tuition includes:

  • Professional child care
  • All-day meals (breakfast, soup, main course, afternoon snack)
  • 24-hour accident insurance
  • Implementation of an educational program consistent with the Ministry of Education curriculum for all age groups
  • English language lessons in groups of up to 10 children: three times a week
  • German language activities for 4, 5, and 6-year-olds
  • Music education classes (rhythmics) – twice a week
  • General development gymnastics with corrective elements – twice a week
  • Speech therapy
  • Psychological care
  • Religion classes
  • Participation in theater performances and concerts held at the preschool

The preschool is open daily from Monday to Friday from 6:30 AM to 5:30 PM. Please note that the preschool is closed on Good Friday and Christmas Eve.
