How to Reach Us

By Car

From the city center, drive along Świdnicka Street towards the Market Square. Łaciarska Street is located in the Old Town area, near the Market Square.

By Public Transport

Multiple bus and tram lines to the city center - stop 'Rynek' or 'Świdnicka' (a few minutes walk).

Parking

Paid street parking and nearby parking lots available in the city center.
