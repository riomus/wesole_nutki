Winter Fun in the Snow

Opublikowano 15 stycznia 2025

Winter is in full swing, and our preschoolers couldn’t wait to play in the snow! As soon as the first snow fell, we went out to the playground to fully enjoy the winter.

What did we do?

The children had the opportunity to:

  • Build snowmen
  • Have snowball fights (safely, of course!)
  • Make snow angels
  • Build snow castles
